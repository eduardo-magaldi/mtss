the talking cat

the girl was gōing fōr a walk. shē met a fat cat. “can cats talk?” the girl said.

the cat said, “I can talk, but I do not talk to girls. I talk to dogs.”

the girl did not līke that cat. “I do not līke cats that will not talk to mē.”

the cat said, “I will not talk to girls.”

the girl said, “I do not līke that cat. and I do not give fish cāke to cats I do not līke.”

the cat said, “I līke fish cāke, sō I will talk to this girl.”

sō the girl and the cat āte the fish cāke.

the end
