pāint that nōse1
a fat dog met a little dog.
the fat dog had a red nōse.2 the little dog had a red nōse.3
the fat dog said, “I have a red nōse.”
the little dog said, “I wish I did not have a red nōse.”4
the fat dog got a can of pāint. he said, “pāint that nōse.”5
sō the little dog did pāint her nōse. shē said, “now this nōse is not red.”
shē kissed the fat dog on the ēar. now the fat dog has pāint on his ēar.6
the end
